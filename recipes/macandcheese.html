Mac and Cheese

Mac and Cheese - A super easy recipe for the kids.

Ingredients

  • 1 box Kraft mac and cheese
  • 1 cup milk
  • (optional) hot dogs

Instructions

  1. Bring medium saucepan of water to boil
  2. Ensure you have removed cheese packet from the box, then add the noodles to the boiling water. Stir occassionally.
  3. Once noodles are desired firmness, remove from heat and drain with colander.
  4. Put drained noodles back into the saucepan.
  5. Add 1 cup milk and cheese seasoning to the saucepan and stir over low-medium heat.
  6. If desired, also add chopped and cooked hotdog pieces.
  7. Enjoy!
