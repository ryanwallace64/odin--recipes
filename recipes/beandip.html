Bean Dip

Bean Dip - A quick and easy recipe for gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground turkey or hamburger
  • 1 15oz can of refried beans
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 1 medium green pepper
  • (optional) jalapeno peppers

Instructions

  1. Dice the onion, green pepper, and jalapeno pepper(if used).
  2. Brown the ground turket or hamburger.
  3. Remove any excess grease from the pan.
  4. Add all veggies and cook over medium heat for two minutes.
  5. Follow the directions on your taco seasoning packet. This usually involves adding ~2/3 cups water, bringing to boil, then sauteeing on low heat for a few minutes.
  6. After taco seasoning directions are complete, add the entire can of refried beans.
  7. Over low heat, stir all ingredients to mix the beans with the meat mixture.
  8. Use the dip for any kind of mexican food base such as taco, tostadas, burritos, or simply dip chips in it and Enjoy!
