Spaghetti

Spaghetti - One of the simplest recipes for a new cook.

    Ingredients
  • 1 lb ground turkey or hamburger
  • 1 box of spaghetti pasta
  • 1 bottle of spaghetti sauce
  • (optional) frozen breadsticks

Part 1: The Pasta

  1. Boil a pot of water and place the pasta in the pot, fully submerging them.
  2. Once the noodles are the desired firmness, remove from heat, and drain the water using a colander.
  3. Place the noodles back in the now dry pot.

Part 2: The Sauce

  1. In a medium sized skillet, brown a pound of ground turkey or hamburger.
  2. Once browned, drain any excess grease in the pan if you're health-concious.
  3. Pour in a bottle of spaghetti sauce. I recommend Prego Italian sausage and garlic flavor.
  4. Heat the skillet to desired sauce temperature, then serve over your cooked pasta.
  5. Enjoy!
